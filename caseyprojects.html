Sandals

Sandals is an Android App that allows for user friendly group chats and status updates. Customize your groups and post whatever you feel like! You can also share your feelings and let your friends know how you are doing!

Tapstr2: Swole Uprising

The story begins with BombSquad Sam taking to the skies in an attempt to defeat the endless waves of bombs that he failed to defuse in Tapstr1. The gameplay involves the player selecting the correct colored bullet to shoot at the incoming enemies.

Tapstr

Tapstr is a game aimed to test a player's reaction skills and hand eye coordinations. The Indie game was inspired by Twister as the user uses four fingers to defuse bombs! Tapstr is currently Version 1.2

Imgur
