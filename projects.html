Animated Pomodoro

Inspired by the Pomodoro Technique, I made my own website that resembles a Pomodoro time management system (Of course, I kinda tried to make it stylish... but I hope it looks bright and cool?). This technique uses a timer to alternate between intervals of work and intervals of rest. The user can also customize the length of each Pomodoro.
